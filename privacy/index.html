Privacy Policy

Vamo: Padel Score Buddy is designed as an on-device scorekeeping app for padel.

Data Stored On Device

The app may store the following information locally on your device:

  • Current match state, team names, scoring options, and app settings.
  • Finished match history.
  • Photos you choose for match share cards.
  • Share-card text or sticker overlays you add.

Data Collection

We do not collect, transmit, sell, or track personal data from the app. Vamo does not require an account and does not send match data to a server.

Photos

The app asks for photo library access only when you choose to add a match photo or save a share image. Photos are used to create match result cards.

Apple Watch

When using the Apple Watch companion app, score state may be sent between your iPhone and Apple Watch using Apple WatchConnectivity. This data stays within your devices.

Third-Party Services

The app does not include third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the developer through the support page.
